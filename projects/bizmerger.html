Bizmerger

BizMerger is an online platform that connects buyers, sellers, and investors across business ventures and corporate transactions. It serves as a marketplace and facilitator for business takeovers, mergers and acquisitions, joint ventures, and related consultancy services, with a focus on opportunities in Singapore, Malaysia, and Southeast Asia, while supporting global participation.

Features

  • Business Listing & Deal Matching
  • M&A and Takeover Services
  • Corporate Consultancy Support
  • Cross-Border Opportunities
  • Real Estate Integration

Tech Stack

Ruby on Rails Hotwire PostgreSQL Heroku AWS
